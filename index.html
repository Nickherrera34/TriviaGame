Nick Herrera Trivia Game

Ever wonder how Nick got here? Ever wonder what his interests are? You're in luck! This game challenges you to judge a book by its cover and discover what you think you know about your current student. Unfortunately, I am not that interesting so this wont be the most exciting quiz you'll ever take. Either way, good luck!

You will have 30 seconds to answer as many questions as you can. Hit the Start button to begin.

  5. Has Nick ever tried sushi?
  6. What rapper does Nick passionately dislake? (and when I say dislike, I actually mean hate)
  7. What is the best show on TV (This is not an opinion, this is fact?)
